The Cloud9-PH Story

Born in the heart of the Philippines, Cafe Cloud9-PH started with a simple mission: to elevate the local coffee experience. We source our beans from the highlands of Benguet and Sagada, ensuring every cup supports local farmers.


Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or simply escape the hustle, we provide the perfect blend of comfort and quality.
